About Us

A Taste of Cordova’s Tradition

For decades, Entoy’s Bakasihan in Buagsong, Cordova has served locals and travelers alike with its signature dish — the Bakasi. Prepared with fresh catch and slow-cooked Cebuano style, it’s more than food — it’s heritage. Our restaurant takes pride in keeping the authentic taste of Cordova’s coastal culture alive for generations to come.
